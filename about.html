CREATIVE COMMON
GLOBAL SUBMIT 2023

The global submit brings together the community of experts, academics and activist who comprise the Creative Commons affiliate network in a different country every Two year.
This year we hope to expand our invitation list including the organisation and individual who wants to work with us on shared projects that are advanced the cause of the Common, free culture and open knowledge
So if you're active and engaged in the world of open content and knowledge - free software advocates , wikipediaans, open knowledge, galleries, liberies, museums, archives, goverment and foundation, lawyer and activist - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant common together.
